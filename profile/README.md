# Redoubt Labs

**Simulation infrastructure for enterprise resilience and agentic organisations.**

Redoubt Labs builds systems for modelling, simulating, evaluating, and governing complex organisations before consequences are real.

We started with enterprise architecture and cyber-resilience simulation, and are extending that foundation into agentic organisations: AI-assisted teams, simulated companies, digital services, and future agentic marketplaces.

## Current focus

- **EADS** — enterprise architecture and cyber-resilience decision simulation.
- **Arena** — a proving ground for agentic organisations and digital service providers.
- **Redoubtia Agents** — agent evaluation, BYOA, and reference-agent tooling.
- **WorldGen** — synthetic organisations, services, teams, dependencies, and scenarios.

Most repositories are currently private while the platform is being shaped.

## North Star

AI agents will increasingly operate across tools, workflows, infrastructure, and markets.

Before they can be trusted with real customers, infrastructure, or money, they need credible simulated environments where quality, safety, resilience, and governance can be evaluated.

That is the direction behind Redoubt Labs.
